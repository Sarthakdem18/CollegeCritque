About DTU Delhi

Delhi Technological University (DTU), previously known as Delhi College of Engineering, is a top-tier engineering university in India. Known for its legacy, excellent faculty, and research-oriented approach, DTU consistently ranks among the top engineering institutes in India.

Highlights

Reputation

DTU is recognized as one of the oldest engineering colleges in India, with a rich history and strong reputation for excellence in engineering and technology.

Placements

Top companies such as Google, Microsoft, and Tata recruit DTU students with competitive packages. The university has an effective placement cell to support students.

Research and Innovation

DTU emphasizes research and development, providing students with opportunities to work on projects across various engineering fields and collaborate with industries.

Student Life

DTU has an active student culture, with numerous clubs and societies, as well as major events like Engifest and Techweek that showcase student talents and technical skills.

Academic Offerings

DTU offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fields like Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, and Mechanical Engineering, delivered by distinguished faculty members.
