About IIT Bombay

The Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay) is one of the top engineering institutions in India. Known for its academic excellence, innovative research, and exceptional faculty, IIT Bombay attracts some of the brightest students from across the country and the world.

Student Life

The IIT Bombay campus offers a vibrant student life with cultural and technical festivals like Mood Indigo and Techfest, along with numerous clubs and societies.

Academic Offerings

IIT Bombay offers a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in fields like Engineering, Design, Management, and Pure Sciences, delivered by accomplished faculty members.

Campus Facilities

IIT Bombay provides modern facilities such as state-of-the-art labs, a central library, advanced hostels, recreational centers, and sports complexes to foster a well-rounded campus life.
