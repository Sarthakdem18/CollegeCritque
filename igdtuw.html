About IGTUW

Indira Gandhi Technical University for Women (IGTUW) is a leading technical institution dedicated to empowering women through quality education in engineering and technology. Established in 1998, IGTUW is located in Delhi, India, and is renowned for its strong emphasis on research, innovation, and holistic development for female students.

Highlights

Empowering Women

IGTUW is focused on providing women with opportunities in the field of technical education, with a focus on closing the gender gap in STEM disciplines.

Research and Innovation

The university promotes a research-centric approach, encouraging students to participate in innovative projects and collaborations with industries and academia.

Industry Exposure

IGTUW offers excellent industry exposure, with tie-ups with leading companies, internships, and projects that help students gain practical experience.

Student Life

The university boasts a vibrant campus life, with numerous cultural, technical, and sports events organized throughout the year, fostering all-round development of students.

Academic Offerings

IGTUW offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, technology, and management, alongside a variety of skill-building courses to prepare students for the job market.

Campus Facilities

The campus is equipped with modern laboratories, libraries, hostels, and recreational facilities, ensuring a well-rounded environment for academic and extracurricular activities.
